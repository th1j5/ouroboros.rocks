A decentralized packet network

{{< /blocks/cover >}} {{% blocks/lead %}} Ouroboros stems from our deep interest in computer networks, tackling some long standing problems in TCP/IP networks such as achieving clean fragmentation, routing scalability, efficient congestion control and simple multicast. Instead of trying to tackle these issues in isolation, we subscribe to the view that the entire TCP/IP design is *fundamentally* broken, and a different approach is needed to build efficient packet networks. {{% /blocks/lead %}} {{< blocks/section >}} Ouroboros is **free software**..
